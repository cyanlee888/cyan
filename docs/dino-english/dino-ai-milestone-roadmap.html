Positioning · Who We Are

A real-time, interactive AI English class for children aged 3–12 in non-English-speaking countries — an AI avatar teacher delivers a structured English-learning experience where kids speak up and get feedback in every lesson, at about 1/10 the price of a live tutor.

Target Users

  • The buyer: parents who value their child's English education but are put off by the price or scheduling hassle of human tutors
  • The user: children aged 3–12 (the product style is shifting from mixed-age toward kid-focused)
  • Launch markets: Korea & Saudi Arabia (T0 pricing ~$9.99)
North Star · MPW
Meaningful Minutes per Week — real learning minutes, not vanity DAU

Differentiation · Why Us

Human 1-on-1 tutors1/10 the price, available anytime, no booking; the AI teacher has memory and consistency
Pre-recorded learning appsnot tap-through videos but real-time conversation — the child speaks and the teacher instantly responds, corrects, and encourages (Teaching Moves)
General AI chat productsa structured 864-lesson curriculum mapped to CEFR/GSE, plus placement tests and learning reports — a "course," not a "chat toy"

Moats, Ranked

  • Teaching-move engine (the Thinker model — from "can chat" to "can teach")
  • Proprietary curriculum & placement data
  • Custom TTS / Avatar kid appeal
  • Post-class practice & gamification ecosystem

The first two are real moats; the last two are experience amplifiers.
